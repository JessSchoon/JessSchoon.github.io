“You and Winnie the Pooh have always had a special connection” -My mom

A brief overview:

My husband and I were married in 2010 after only 10 months of knowing each other, bought our house in 2012, had our daughter in 2013, and are expecting our son this summer. Moving forward we have no idea what will be happening. We try to constantly have our ears and hearts open to listen to what God would have us do in our lives. Sometimes listening in this way includes driving across the country with stuff in storage not knowing where our final destination will be.

The above quote should tell you quite a bit about my life. It is constantly progressing, changing, and trying the value of family and friendship. My family and I are constantly “On Pooh’s Grand Adventure”. I grew up in Texas, my husband grew up in Pennsylvania, we met outside of Boston, bought a house in Rhode Island before moving to California, and finally we are currently living in Pennsylvania. Where will the spirit lead us next?

This website is meant to be a comedic log of our adventures while being a place to gather strength from each other on points that aren’t completely obvious to everyone. Please enjoy as stories are shared of life with a happy baby and an engineer.
